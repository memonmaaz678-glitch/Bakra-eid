Eid al-Adha, also known as Bakra Eid, is an important Islamic festival celebrated by Muslims around the world. It honors the sacrifice and obedience of Prophet Ibrahim (A.S.) to Allah. On this day, Muslims offer Eid prayers, sacrifice animals like goats or cows, and share the meat with family, friends, and the poor. The festival teaches lessons of faith, kindness, and generosity. bakra
